About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Department of Mechanics and Engineering Science, Peking University (2017.9 - )

My academic research interests: microstructure mechanics, strain gradient viscoelasticity, finite element method.

If you are interested in my research, do not hesitate to contact me. My e-mail, linzy AT pku.edu.cn

 

Also, I am interested in artificial intelligence (AI). I did some works in computer vision during 2017 - 2020. My GitHub: https://github.com/daniallin, maybe you can find something useful.
